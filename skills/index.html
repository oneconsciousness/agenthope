The six skills

Hope is six skills your AI runs in order. Each is a plain-language set of instructions — open one to read it, or point the AI you already use straight at it.

Step 1

Onboarding

Tell Hope your story. It gathers what you already have and builds your career file — on your computer.

 Step 2

Portfolio

Hope's signature skill — a designed page recruiters stop for, with a living timeline of your career.

 Step 3

Publish

Put it on the web — one shareable link you own. Hope carries the whole technical load for you.

 Step 4

Skill Gap

The honest truth about where you stand against a real role — ranked gaps, a plan, and your strengths named too.

 Step 5

Proof Projects

The one or two projects that close your biggest gaps fastest — with a build plan, not just an idea.

 Step 6

Dashboard

Your whole hunt on one private page — the destination, the proof, the gap, the plan, Hope's take.
