The three skills

Hope is three skills your AI runs in order. Each is a plain-language set of instructions — open one to read it, or point the AI you already use straight at it.

Step 1

Onboarding

Tell Hope your story. It gathers what you already have and builds your career file — on your computer.

 Step 2

Portfolio

Hope's signature skill — a designed page recruiters stop for, with a living timeline of your career.

 Step 3

Publish

Put it on the web — one shareable link you own. Hope carries the whole technical load for you.

What's new

Hope keeps getting better at getting you hired. The latest improvements, in plain words:

  • Jun 2026

    Hope now learns your goal and your timeline.

    When you start, it asks what role you're aiming for, how much time you can give the hunt, and how much runway you have. A few months and a few weeks call for very different plans — now yours actually fits your life, not a generic template.

  • Jun 2026

    Your wins finally stand out on your portfolio.

    Scholarships, honors, and standout achievements now show up as highlights on your education — not just a degree and a date. The things you're proud of get the spotlight they earned.

  • Jun 2026

    Ready-to-paste instructions for the AI you already use.

    Copy a tuned set of instructions for Claude or ChatGPT straight from the home page and go — no guesswork about what to say.
