# Frequently asked questions

<div class="faq">
  <details class="faq-item" open>
    <summary>Is JobHunt with Hope free?</summary>
    <p class="faq-a">Yes. JobHunt with Hope is completely free and open-source under the MIT license. There is no paid tier, no account, and no credit card — you use the AI agent you already have.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Is my data safe and private?</summary>
    <p class="faq-a">Yes. Your career facts live in a single file on your own computer. There is no tracking and no account. Nothing leaves your machine except the portfolio page you explicitly choose to publish.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Do I need to know how to code?</summary>
    <p class="faq-a">No coding is required. You talk to your AI agent in plain language and it builds the portfolio and resume for you.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Which AI agents does it work with?</summary>
    <p class="faq-a">It works with the AI agents most people already use, including ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. You can install it as a Claude plugin, or point any capable assistant at the open skills.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>What do I get at the end?</summary>
    <p class="faq-a">Three things: a designed portfolio website with a playable career timeline, a recruiter-ready resume PDF that survives applicant tracking systems, and one free shareable link in your name that you can paste anywhere.</p>
  </details>
  <details class="faq-item">
    <summary>Will it actually get me hired?</summary>
    <p class="faq-a">No tool can promise that — and anyone who does is selling you something. What Hope does do is get your real work in front of people, looking sharp, today. That part is in your hands, and it is free.</p>
  </details>
</div>
