Frequently asked questions

Is Job Hunt with Hope free?

Yes. Job Hunt with Hope is completely free and open-source under the MIT license. There is no paid tier, no account, and no credit card — you use the AI agent you already have.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes. Your career facts live in a single file on your own computer. There is no tracking and no account. Nothing leaves your machine except the portfolio page you explicitly choose to publish.

Do I need to know how to code?

No coding is required. You talk to your AI agent in plain language — hand it a résumé, your LinkedIn, or your GitHub, or just describe your work — and it builds the portfolio and résumé for you.

Which AI agents does it work with?

It works with the AI agents most people already use, including ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini. You can install it as a Claude plugin, or point any capable assistant at the open skills.

What do I get at the end?

Three things: a designed portfolio website with a playable career timeline, a recruiter-ready résumé PDF that survives applicant tracking systems, and one free shareable link in your name that you can paste anywhere.

Will it actually get me hired?

No tool can promise that. What Hope does is get your work in front of people, looking sharp, today — and 85.5% of early users landed multiple interviews. It costs you nothing to try.
